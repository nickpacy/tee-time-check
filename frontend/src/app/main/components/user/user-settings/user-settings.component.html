Torrey Pines Login Info

Feature: Automatic Tee Time Holding (Beta)

  • When a tee time matching your preferences is found, the system will automatically use your login to hold the tee time for 5 minutes, giving you a brief window to secure it.
  • During this 5-minute window, the tee time will not be visible to you or others.
  • The tee time will reappear exactly 5 minutes after it's held.

By enabling this feature, you enhance your chances of getting your desired tee time, especially during peak hours. Please make sure to act promptly after receiving a notification to avoid missing out!
